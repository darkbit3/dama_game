❓ How to Play
🎯 Objective

Capture all of your opponent's pieces — or leave them with no legal moves — to win the game.

♟ Movement
  • Pieces move diagonally forward one square at a time.
  • You can only move on the dark squares.
  • Tap a piece to select it, then tap a highlighted square to move.
⚔️ Capturing
  • Jump over an opponent's piece to capture it — the landing square must be empty.
  • If a capture is available, you must take it.
  • Chain multiple captures in a single turn when possible (multi-jump).
👑 King (Dama)
  • Reach the opponent's back row to promote your piece to a King (Dama).
  • Kings move and capture in all four diagonal directions.
  • In Ethiopian Dama, kings can fly long distances — they are very powerful!
💰 Betting
  • Set your bet amount before challenging another player.
  • The winner takes the full pot from both players.
  • Make sure you have enough balance before entering a match.
🤖 vs AI
  • Tap vs AI to practice against one of 15 bots.
  • Bots range from Very Easy to Very Hard.
  • AI games do not cost any ETB — perfect for practice!
